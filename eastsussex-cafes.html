Pooh Corner

Pooh Corner is situated on the edge of the Ashdown Forest in Hartfield, East Sussex. The perfect stop for either the casual visitor or the die hard fan of A.A. Milne’s most famous creation, Winnie the Pooh and his friends whose stories came to life just a short walk away in the 100 Acre Wood! So whether you come for a drink or something to eat (honey is always on the menu!) you’ll find yourself busy doing nothing in no time!

Opening hours

Monday - Saturday 10:00 - 5:00

Sundays and Bank Holidays 11:00 - 4:00

  • Food Cakes, ice cream and sandwiches available
  • Outside seating Outside seating area available
  • Dogs welcome Dogs are welcome in the tea garden
  • Public transport Bus stops in the village

Hanushka Coffee House

Over second and third cups flow matters of high finance, high state, common gossip and low comedy. Coffee is a social binder, a warmer of tongues, a soberer of minds, a stimulant of wit, a foiler of sleep if you want it so. From roadside mugs to the classic demitasse, it is the perfect democrat.

At a glance
  • Food Sandwiches, Paninis, Salads and Cakes
  • Outside seating Seating area in front of the cafe

Bluebells Tearoom

Bluebells Tearoom is situated in the historic town of Battle and surrounded by beautiful countryside. Explore the local countryside or have a wonder round the Battle Museum of Local History or the Battle Abbey, then come in and enjoy one of our delicious cakes or afternoon tea and catch up with friends.

At a glance
  • Food Cakes and Afternoon tea
  • Outside seating Seating area in front of the cafe
  • Public transport Battle train station is only a short walk
