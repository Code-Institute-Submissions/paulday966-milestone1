The Dabbling Duck

The Dabbling Duck café restaurant situated in the picturesque village of Shere, a few minutes from Guildford and within the Surrey Hills area of outstanding natural beauty. We are open 7 days a week for breakfast, coffee & cakes, lunch and afternoon tea - served either inside the café or in our large patio garden. During the winter months, customers can enjoy dining in our yurt, warmed by our log burning stove. We enjoy a wide customer base, so whether you are meeting friends for coffee, lunching with your family, having a business meeting, walking, cycling - you will be assured of a warm welcome by our friendly staff.

      Cromwells

      A warm Cranleigh welcome to our charming Cromwell Coffee House situated in the centre of the village and set behind an attractive flower filled patio. Oliver House is a delightful, beamed building dating from the 1560s, steeped in history. We offer an extensive menu from breakfasts and lunches to delicious homemade cakes and cream teas, which you can enjoy outdoors on the patio or around our cosy Inglenook fireplace.
