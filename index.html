Welcome to Quirky Cafes

This website is for everyone that would like to find out about friendly and welcoming cafes in the beautiful countryside and bustling seaside towns of East Sussex, Kent and Surrey. Each cafe has contact details, opening hours and a description about each unique cafe. Google Maps shows each cafes location, which can be used for driving directions.

East Sussex

Pooh Corner is surrounded by beautiful countryside with plenty of options for long and short country walks to help build up an appetite for the wonderful selection of cakes on offer

Hanushka Coffee House is located in the seaside town of Hastings. After a wonder around the town, or a swim in the sea come on in and have a cake and some coffee

Bluebells Tearoom is in the middle of Battle opposite Battle Abbey. After a walk in the lovely countryside surrounding the town, or a visit to the local museums, come and enjoy some afternoon tea

Kent

Shoreham Aircraft Museum and Tearooms is surrounded by lovely countryside with lots of options for country walks or bicycle rides to build up an appetite for the cakes and snacks on offer

Steep Street Coffee House is in Folkestone and only minutes away from the beach and harbour and a selection of art studios and boutique shops to explore before settling down to a delicious cake and a natter

Surrey

The Dabbling Duck is surrounded by the Surrey Hills area of outstanding natural beauty, the perfect place for a walk or bike ride. When your done come in for a well-earned tasty slice of cake and a coffee

Headley Village Stores and Tea Room is a great stop off for cake and coffee when out cycling in the Surrey Hills or walking in the lovely countryside

After exploring the local countryside by foot or bike or having a wonder around the town come in for some cake or a snack
